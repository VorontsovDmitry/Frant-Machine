About Frant

Frant Machine is one of the brightest and most interesting magazines dedicated to the topic of motorsport. We are the very first to cover all the news from the world of sports cars and motorcycles, as well as publish articles about the use of cars or the history of the automotive industry.

We are able to please with our work both any petrolhead and a complete beginner.

Frant Machine offices are located in most automotive capitals of the world: Stuttgart and Munich (Germany), Detroit (USA), Toyota City (Japan), Turin (Italy), etc.

Our staff consists of over 70 people: 20 journalists, 6 photographers, 15 professional racing drivers, etc.

The digital edition of Frant Machine is absolutely free, but you can support us for any amount.

You can purchase the printed edition of Frant Machine in all popular stores in your country or subscribe to the home mailing list.
